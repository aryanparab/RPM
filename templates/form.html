Online KYC verification

Due to unfortunate circumstances, verification of KYC cannot be done in person at your preferred branch of bank. Instead we can verify your KYC details through online mode. Please follow the following steps to ensure KYC verification.

1

Upload your documents.

Upload clear, scanned images in JPG format of your official government documents as instructed.

2

Video KYC

To ensure the person uploading the documents is actually you, please follow the steps required for video KYC verification.

3

OTP verification

An OTP will be sent to the mobile number you have registered with which must be submitted to finish the entire KYC process.
